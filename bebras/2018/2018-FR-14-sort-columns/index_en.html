Sort Columns

Clicking on a "sort" button changes the order of the columns so that On the row of the clicked button, the small spheres are placed to the left of the big spheres. On the row of the clicked button, the spheres are sorted from the smallest on the left, to the biggest on the right. On the row of the clicked button, the spheres are sorted by increasing order.

During this operation, each column is moved as little as possible.

Click on the buttons to place the columns in the correct order, as shown on the objective.

To obtain a full score, do this in 3 steps.
