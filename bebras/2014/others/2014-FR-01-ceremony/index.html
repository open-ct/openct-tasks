Ceremony

The Beaver community has a very strict protocol for organizing ceremonies, represented by the illustration above. Each text represent a possible step of a ceremony.

An arrow between two steps means that if both steps are performed during a ceremony, then the step at the start of the arrow has to be performed at some point before the step at the end of the arrow.

Drag the steps on the right to change their order, so that the final order corresponds to the rules defined by the illustration
