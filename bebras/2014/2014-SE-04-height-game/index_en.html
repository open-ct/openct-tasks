Paragliding

ThreeFourFive beavers are making paragliding. A beaver can fly higher than the other beavers.
A beaver may also be in front of other beavers.

Beavers transmit radio information about their position in the sky.

  • Castor A : « I am in front of two beavers. »
  • Castor B : « There is no beaver higher than me ; and there is a beaver behind me. »
  • Castor C : « One beaver is higher than me and another one is lower than me. »
  • Castor A : « There are 2 beavers higher than me and 1 lower than me. »
  • Castor B : « There is no beaver in front of me. »
  • Castor C : « There are 3 beavers lower than me: 1 in front of me and 2 behind me.. »
  • Castor D : « There are 3 beavers in front of me and no beaver lower than me. »
  • Beaver A : « There are 3 beavers higher than me : 1 in front of me, and 2 behind me. »
  • Beaver B : « There are 4 beavers higher than me : 3 in front of me, and 1 behind me. »
  • Beaver C : « There is only one beaver higher than me, and it is before me. »
  • Beaver D : « There is no beaver higher than me. »
  • Beaver E : « There are only 2 beavers higher than me, and the two are in front of me. »

Can you find the position of the beavers in the sky ?
